Welcome to 108@Nautica

Luxury accommodation in Mossel Bay. Classic contemporary style 2-bedroom en-suite apartment with ocean views from the balcony and only 5 minutes walk to the beach. This designer apartment is situated on the beachfront and is beautifully decorated, fully equipped with all modern conveniences.

Facilities

Below is a list of facilities available at 108 @ Nautica:
  • Two bedrooms both en-suite, sleeping 4
  • Both bathrooms consist of oversize shower, toilet and basin
  • Air conditioner in lounge
  • Lounge with TV, DVD and DSTV dish (bring your own smart card)
  • SMOKING ON BALCONY
  • Secure and exclusive shaded parking bay
  • 24 hour security
  • All linen provided
  • All towels & beach towels provided
  • Washing machine in unit
  • Braai on balcony
  • Serviced daily or weekly (at additional cost)

Activities

  • Golf - Mossel Bay Golf Club, Pinnacle Point, Oubaai, Fancourt Country Club Estate.
